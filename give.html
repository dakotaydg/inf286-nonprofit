Donate to Petals Pantry & Boutique

Due to COVID, we are only accepting NEW & almost new items.

Acceptable new/almost new donation items:
-Clothing, Books, Coats
-Gloves, Hats, Scarves
-Socks, shoes, Bibs, blankets, comforters

RBC does not accept used hair accessories, undergarments breast pumps, bottles, strollers, car seats, teddy bears, cribs, high chairs, and/or any items that may have an expiration date.

Almost New is defined as clean, no tears or holes, no stains, color faded, distressed. For toys: new packaging, all parts, clean with no stains.
